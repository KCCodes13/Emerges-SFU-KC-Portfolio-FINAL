Background



KC Johan graduated from The University of California, San Diego (UCSD) in 2017 with a Bachelor's in Sociology (Emphasis in Culture & Communications). In 2016, she worked her first marketing internship where she learned about social media marketing, lead generating, and being a Brand Ambassador. After graduating in San Diego, she pursued her marketing career. Along the way, she also did other works, temping for front desk positions which built her administrative skills over the years. She made her way along in the industry, working positions as a Social Media Assistant and an Account Coordinator, and developed an interest in blogging, SEO Marketing, and social media marketing.

More recently, in 2021, she completed a Marketing Assistant internship where she learned many valuable skills in WordPress, blog writing, SEO and Google Ads and Asana. After her internship ended, she now works as a Project Assistant part-time while also pursuing her certifications in Digital Marketing, UI / UX Design and Web Development as well as completing materials for her project portfolio.

When she isn't pursuing her career, KC enjoys traveling, photography, cooking, watching movies, and learning new things online.
